🌸 Narrative Resonance

These foundations are not just academic scaffolding. They are the **story of storytelling itself**—how humans have theorized narrative, how technology now participates in that ancient craft, what writers actually need, and how to build tools that honor both precision and the living act of creation.

The Storyweaver Kit is not novel because it invents new structure. It is novel because it **synthesizes** known structures (Vogler, McKee, Cron) into orchestrated agents that respect storytellers' autonomy, cultural boundaries, and the irreplaceable human act of choosing what happens next.
